Battle Gameplay

Similar to Pokemon but you have HS mana as stamina. Every round you are gaining mana, but you are starting around 1-2 mana per monster.

Axes of game design : Degrees of Freedom*

Hard Designs

  • Base Stats [HP,ATK,SPATK,DEF,SPDEF,SPEED] : 2
    • Idea for names: [Health, Strength, Intelligence, Body, Mind, Agility], Can be tuned with an EV equivalent, but is faster than other game training
  • Typings : 2
    • Enough to create uneven-ness in the type matrix.
  • Stamina base, Stamina Gain : 2
    • Max Stamina should range from 5-10ish? Stamina Gain should be 0-2 per turn
  • Abilities : 5
    • Really defines a monster and how to utilize it.
  • Moves : 3
    • More variety with Stamina now. Powerful sttacks with high stamina to offset being able to use them asap.

* Degrees of freedom [0-5] refers to the amount of impact the precieved axis will be designed to have on the gameplay. The higher the numnber, the higher the impact.

Ideas

Abilities (Click to Exapnd)

Quick Reaction - Damage recieved on the turn switching in is halved.

Special - The Opponent's STAB is ignored when they deal damage to you.

Athletic - STA requirements of attacks are reduced by 1.

Type Swap - X type moves are Y type.

3rd type - X type moves get STAB.

Copycat - Gain the effects of the item that the enemy is holding.

Purity - increase all stats by 2x if user started without Item.

Battle of Brawns - All damage from user calculates using users Strength stat and opponents Body stat.

Battle of Wits - All damage from user calculates using users Intelligence stat and opponents Mind stat.

Death Effect - When user dies apply x.

Simple - Increase all stats if you only have 1 move.

Split - Doubles only - When ally is knocked out you then halve your hp and create a clone of yourself in the other slot.

Berserk - Start with 10 stamina however once your stamina hits 0 you faint. No Stamina gain per turn.
