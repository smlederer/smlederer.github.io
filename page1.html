Battle Gameplay

Similar to Pokemon but you have HS mana as stamina. Every round you are gaining mana, but you are starting around 1-2 mana per monster.

Axes of game design : Degrees of Freedom*

Hard Designs

  • Base Stats [HP,ATK,SPATK,DEF,SPDEF,SPEED] : 2
    • Can be tuned with an EV equivalent
  • Typings : 2
    • Enough to create uneven-ness in the type matrix.
  • Stamina base, Stamina Gain : 2
    • Max Stamina should range from 5-10ish? Stamina Gain should be 0-2 per turn
  • Abilities : 5
    • Really defines a monster and how to utilize it.
  • Moves : 3
    • More variety with Stamina now. Powerful sttacks with high stamina to offset being able to use them asap.

* Degrees of freedom [0-5] refers to the amount of impact the precieved axis will be designed to have on the gameplay. The higher the numnber, the higher the impact.

Soft Designs (Hidden Information)

  • Moves
  • Base Stat Tuning
  • Some Abilities
  • Actions During the Turn submitted blind
